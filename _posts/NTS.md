### NTS

[Theodor Meynert](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Theodor_Meynert), 1833-1892, German-Austrian psychiatrist, neuropathologist, and anatomist, according to the book "The Role of the Nucleus of the Solitary Tract in Gustatory Processing", he is the earliest author to name the tract the solitäres Bündel (solitary bundle) in his book "The brain of mammals" published in 1872.
