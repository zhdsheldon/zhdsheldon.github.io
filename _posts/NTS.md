### NTS

[Theodor Meynert](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Theodor_Meynert), 1833-1892, German-Austrian psychiatrist, neuropathologist, and anatomist, according to the book ["The Role of the Nucleus of the Solitary Tract in Gustatory Processing"](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK1840/), he is the earliest author to name the tract the solitäres Bündel (solitary bundle) in his book "The brain of mammals" published in 1872.
