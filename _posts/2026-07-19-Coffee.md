### Coffee bean experience


1. Blue bottle-espresso, chocolate, nougal, and brown sugar: Used for expresso, It is Ok, personally I do not like this combination of chocolate, better add milk so that it is less bitter. 6.5/10, bought from Amazon.

2. Lavazza Qualita Oro, medium roast, Arabica: Used for pour-over without any milk, use ICELANDIC glacial pH 8.4 water, pretty good, good smell, maybe a little bit sweet, still good after cooling. 8/10, bought from Amazon.
